    About Us

    Unit Bahagian Pembangunan & Pengurusan Fasiliti (BPPF) is one of the most important organization in UiTM Cawangan Pahang Kampus Raub. This organization is responsible for managing all things that is related to the development and management of facilities. This organization was established on 15 November 2015. The founder of the organization is UiTM Cawangan Pahang Kampus Raub. The organization are important to the student here as most of the equipment that are often used in programs are mostly from the facility unit.
